§ 01 — Essence i.
Positioning

Real estate, practiced as a discipline — not a hustle.

01

Measured

We underwrite before we imagine. Our instinct is toward precision — numbers that hold up, assumptions that are stated, risks that are named rather than hidden.

02

Local

We invest where we walk. Every submarket — from Marietta to Fayetteville — is evaluated on the ground, not from a dashboard. The Atlanta metro is not a map to us; it's a neighborhood.

03

Collective

Decisions are partnership-level, not personality-driven. Six operators, one framework. What we build we build together and for longer than a cycle.

§ 02 — The Mark ii.

A meridian is the sun's highest point — the instant of exact measurement.

Concept

The mark is literal and quiet. A horizontal line — the meridian, the lot line, the ground — with a single point fixed above it: the apex, the moment of peak value, the sighted coordinate. Two end ticks reference the surveyor's transit. It scales from a favicon to a building sign without loss of character.

Primary Lockup

Mark + wordmark, stacked

The preferred application. Used for formal documents, site header, deal memos, exterior signage. Minimum clear space equal to the mark's height on all sides.

24px digital · 0.75in print

Below this threshold, use the minimal lockup or monogram alone. The apex dot must remain clearly resolved.

§ 02B — Option 2 M°

The Monogram

A letterform that hides a pun — the degree symbol is both the typographic mark of measurement and the apex of the meridian. Modern, restrained, memorable.

M°
§ 02C — Option 3 ⊕

The Coordinate

A surveyor's target or map coordinate. The crosshair marks the point, the rings mark the tolerance. Strong metaphor for precise location and local intelligence.

§ 02D — Option 4 ◉

The Seal

A seal of the partnership. Circular, classical, institutional — the mark of an entity built to last. Conveys permanence and tradition while remaining contemporary.

§ 02E — Option 5 i

The Wordmark

Pure typographic approach. No mark, just the name set with confidence — one italic letter carrying the brand's character. The fashion-house / art-gallery approach.

§ 02F — Option 6 🌐

The Globe

A world with meridian lines. Represents global perspective and geographic expertise. The prime meridian is emphasized, connecting to the surveyor's precision theme.

§ 03 — Palette iii.

Five colors. No more.

A dominant-dark system anchored by obsidian and carried by bone. Brass is the accent — used with restraint to mark importance, never decoration. Fog and ink handle the in-between.
