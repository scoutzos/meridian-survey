01 — The Meridian

Meridian
C O L L E C T I V E

Concept

A surveyor's transit. The meridian line with the sighted apex dot above. Literal, quiet, unmistakably real estate.

Character

The most on-concept of the five. Precision-engineered feel. End ticks reference surveyor tools; the apex dot is the "peak value" moment. Scales beautifully from favicon to building sign.

Best For

A group that wants the brand to quietly signal methodology. Institutional, patient, operator-led.

Positioning

Real estate, practiced as a discipline — not a hustle. Measured, local expertise with a bias toward precision.

Usage Guidelines

Perfect for formal documents, site headers, deal memos, exterior signage. Minimum clear space equal to the mark's height on all sides. Works best against solid backgrounds in high contrast.
